    • About Us

    Janaki Vallabh is a proud authorized marketing partner of India’s No.1 milk brand – AMUL, the world’s largest milk cooperative. Together, we are committed to supporting the backbone of our nation – our dairy farmers.

    In alignment with AMUL’s mission to ensure farmer welfare, Janaki Vallabh brings the best-quality AMUL Cattle Feed to the farmers of Chittaurgarh. Our goal is to empower local dairy farmers by enhancing cattle health, improving milk production, and ultimately increasing their income.

    Driven by trust, quality, and a shared vision for progress, both Janaki Vallabh and AMUL are fully dedicated to the mission of sustainable growth and prosperity for India’s dairy farming community.
